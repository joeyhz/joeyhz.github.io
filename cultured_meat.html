History

In my Senior year of high school I conducted a year long capstone project focused on cultured meat, including a brief documentary and research paper. The project encouraged my already enormous fascination with cultured meat, and in university I went on to volunteer at the New Harvest Conference in 2019, take courses in cellular biology, join the Alternative Protein Project at Cornell, and seek out work on the border of life science and technology to while. While the research I conducted back in high school may not be flawless, I include it here as a record of my long-term passion for this idea.
